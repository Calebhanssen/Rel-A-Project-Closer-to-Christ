I am Enough

Lessons learned

A list of talks that helped me get through this semester

Back to Top

All the lord expects of us is to try, but we really have to try. The people who were healed by Christ were often directed to do something or have something done to them preceding the miracle. This allowed their faith to grow and their testimony to be strengthened. I also was reminded that God will forgive us and truly forget of our sins and iniquities. Howeve,r many mistakes we have made, we have not traveled far enough to avoid the loving arms of Christ and his gospel.

“Come unto Christ and be perfected in him.” Our only hope for true perfection is receiving it as a gift from him. We are reliant upon our savior. This makes us all beggars.

From the beginning of His ministry, Jesus loved the impoverished and the disadvantaged in an extraordinary way. He was born into the home of two of them and grew up among many more of them.

Grace, the enabling power of Jesus Christ, is constant. The grace of Christ is sufficient to cover our debt, sufficient to transform us, and sufficient to help us as long as that transformation takes place. Grace is not the absence of Gods high expectations. Grace is the presence of Gods power.

The pure love of Christ is a gift we are promised when the atonement of Jesus Christ has worked within us. When we begin to align our will with His, we will then know that the pure love of Christ is working within us. He will heal us and purify our souls as we look to Him as our Savior.
