Study Guides

Exploring the Second Half of the New Testament

Introduction

The second half of the New Testament provides profound insights into Christ's teachings, the Atonement, and the growth of the early Church. These study guides offer reflections, scripture connections, and insights into key themes such as grace, faith, and hope.

Key Study Guides

  • Paul’s Letters: Understand themes of grace, redemption, and faith in Romans, Galatians, and Ephesians.
  • The Acts of the Apostles: Explore the courage and unity of the early Church as the gospel spread across nations.
  • Revelation: Discover the hope and assurance found in John’s vision of Christ’s triumph over evil.

Video Resources

Growing in Grace: Discover how Christ’s grace enables transformation and growth in our lives.

Perfection in Christ: Learn how reliance on Christ brings true perfection as a gift of grace.

The Power of Christ’s Love: Reflect on Christ’s unconditional love for all people, especially the disadvantaged.

Insights for Application

As you study these teachings, consider how they apply to your life today:

  • Grace: What does it mean to rely on Christ’s enabling power in daily challenges?
  • Service: How can you emulate Christ’s love by serving those around you?
  • Hope: How do Revelation’s promises inspire faith in difficult times?

Through study and reflection, these guides can deepen your understanding and strengthen your testimony of Christ.
