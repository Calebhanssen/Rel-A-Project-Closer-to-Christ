Growing Closer to Christ

Welcome

This website is a place to deepen understanding of Christ’s teachings from the second half of the New Testament. It is a journey to strengthen faith, apply gospel principles, and draw closer to the Savior through study and reflection.

Key Insights

Paul’s Teachings

Paul's epistles to the Romans, Corinthians, and others explore grace, faith, and love. These writings remind us of the transformative power of Christ’s Atonement and the unity of His followers.

The Acts of the Apostles

Acts recounts the faith, courage, and miracles of the early Apostles as they spread Christ’s gospel. Their examples inspire us to trust in the Lord’s guidance as we face challenges.

Hope in Revelation

The Book of Revelation offers hope and assurance that Christ will triumph over evil. Its vivid imagery reminds us to remain steadfast, trusting in His promises of eternal joy.

Featured Scripture

“For I am persuaded, that neither death, nor life, nor angels, nor principalities, nor powers, nor things present, nor things to come… shall be able to separate us from the love of God, which is in Christ Jesus our Lord.” - Romans 8:38-39
